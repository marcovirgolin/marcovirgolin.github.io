Biography

I am a Senior Data Scientist at Ingka, IKEA, working on making AI trustworhty. Currently, my main focus is on taming generative AI, specifically large language models such as causal transformers.

Feel free to reach out if you need technical advice on explainable AI (fairness, feature attribution, recourse, transparent models), generative AI (large language models, diffusion models), or other ML subjects.

Before my current employment, I've been:

  • Scientific Staff Member in tenure track (the equivalent of a starting professor) at Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI), the national research institute for mathematics and computer science in the Netherlands.
  • Postdoctoral researcher with Mattias Wahde at Chalmers University of Technology.
  • PhD student with Peter A. N. Bosman and Tanja Alderliesten at CWI.
  • Intern and M.Sc. thesis student with with Alberto Bartoli and Eric Medvet at the Machine Learning Lab of the University of Trieste.
