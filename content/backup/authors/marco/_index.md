Marco Virgolin is a postdoctoral fellow at the Applied Artificial Intelligence (AAI) research group of Chalmers University of Technology. His research interests include explainable AI, evolutionary computation and human-machine interaction. He leads the Robotic Neurobiology group, which develops self-reconfiguring robots, systems of self-organizing robots, and mobile sensor networks.
